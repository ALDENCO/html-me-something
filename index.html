I want to connect employers and job seekers with a credible meeting place in which actual, available jobs are listed and kept current.)

  1. My plan is to start by creating a reputable job search site for the state of Colorado.

  3. I then plan to use a deep web research tool that I discovered and utlitized while working with the BBB that provides active email addresses associated with a website.
  4. Finally, I aim to work with local chamber members and/or the BBB to connect their BBB rating and information to the company's job listing, so that objective information can be provided for job seekers and quality employers.

  • My second idea is to create a cheaper sales lead offering that pulls from the SOS API, and other open, free databases like Google Maps and FourSquare to ensure quality database information.
  1. I know for a fact that small businesses are hard up for quality sales leads and I was a lead researcher in the effort to raise the quality of the BBB's database locally.
